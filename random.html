Elon Musk is a fascinating individual with many accomplishments under his belt. Here are some random facts that you may not know about him:

Random Facts

  • Musk taught himself computer programming at the age of 12.
  • He dropped out of Stanford University's Ph.D. program after just two days.
  • His first company, Zip2, was sold for almost $300 million.
  • Musk was the inspiration for the character of Tony Stark/Iron Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
  • He has been involved in several lawsuits over the years, including a defamation lawsuit against a Thai cave rescuer.
  • Musk has stated that he wants to retire on Mars.
