Introduction

Elon Musk is one of the richest people in the world, with a net worth of over $200 billion. With all that money, it's no surprise that he has some extravagant spending habits. Here are some examples of what he spends his money on:

Spending Habits

  • Real estate: Elon Musk owns multiple mansions and properties around the world, including a $70 million mansion in Bel Air, California.
  • Cars: Musk is known for his love of cars and has an impressive collection, including a 1967 Jaguar E-Type, a Lotus Esprit submarine car used in the James Bond movie "The Spy Who Loved Me," and of course, multiple Tesla vehicles.
  • Private jets: Musk owns at least one private jet, a Gulfstream G650ER, which he reportedly purchased for $70 million.
  • Travel: Musk has been to space multiple times with his company SpaceX, and has also taken trips to Antarctica and the North Pole.
  • Philanthropy: While he may spend a lot of money on himself, Musk is also known for his charitable donations. In 2020, he donated $5 million to the Khan Academy, a non-profit organization that provides free online education.
